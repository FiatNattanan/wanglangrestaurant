About us

Welcome to Wang Lang Restaurant, a culinary haven nestled in the heart of downtown Toronto. We take pride in offering an authentic Thai dining experience, boasting a menu that showcases the rich and vibrant flavors of Thailand. Our commitment to delivering original Thai cuisine crafted with the finest ingredients ensures each dish embodies the essence of traditional Thai taste, delighting the senses with every bite. Beyond our delectable food, we invite you to unwind and indulge in our bar, where handcrafted cocktails perfectly complement our exquisite dishes. At Wang Lang, we aim to transport our guests on a gastronomic journey through Thailand, providing an ambiance that exudes warmth, hospitality, and the true spirit of Thai culture. Join us and savor the genuine taste of Thailand right here in the heart of Toronto.
